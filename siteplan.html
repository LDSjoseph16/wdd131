Iligan City: Majestic Waterfalls

Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Iligan City: The City of Majestic Waterfalls

Reasoning: I chose this name because it directly reflects Iligan's official branding and unique geographical identity as the home to over 20 waterfalls.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a central digital hub for tourists and locals to discover the waterfalls of Iligan City. It provides location details, access levels, and a planning tool to encourage eco-tourism in the region.

Scenarios

  • Which waterfalls in Iligan City are the easiest to access for a family with children?
  • How can I save my travel preferences to plan a visit to the falls later?
