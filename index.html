The Power of Iligan: Maria Cristina Falls

Nature's Grandeur Awaits

Iligan City, known as the City of Majestic Waterfalls, boasts over 20 natural falls. From the industrial power of Maria Cristina to the hidden beauty of Tinago, there is an adventure for every traveler.
